Editorial standards

Accuracy before persuasion.

PrimeShop Reviews publishes commercial health and wellness content with an emphasis on clarity, traceability, and realistic expectations.

Accuracy

Material product details should be tied to a current label, manufacturer statement, seller term, or reputable external source. When information cannot be verified, we qualify or omit it.

Health language

We avoid claims that a dietary supplement diagnoses, treats, cures, or prevents disease. Ingredient research is described in context and is not presented as proof of a finished product’s effectiveness.

Editorial independence

Manufacturers and sellers do not receive the right to approve our editorial conclusions. If a company provides information or requests a correction, we evaluate the supporting evidence before making a change.

Affiliate relationships

Some articles contain affiliate links. We disclose that PrimeShop Reviews may earn a commission from qualifying purchases. Compensation does not increase the price paid by the reader.

No fabricated experience

We do not claim personal use, laboratory testing, medical review, customer consensus, or clinical validation unless the article can accurately document it.

Corrections

Confirmed factual errors are corrected as promptly as practical. Material updates should change the displayed revision date. Stylistic edits that do not affect meaning may be made without a correction notice.

Reader responsibility

Our content supports research; it does not make personal healthcare decisions. Readers should review the current label, consider medication and condition risks, and consult an appropriate professional when needed.
